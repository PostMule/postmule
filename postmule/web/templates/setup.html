Connect your Gmail account

PostMule checks your Gmail for mail scan notifications from your virtual mailbox and for bill attachments from billers.

Not your regular Gmail password — see instructions below.

↗ Get an App Password
  1. Sign in to your Google account if prompted
  2. Click "Select app" → choose "Other (custom name)" → type PostMule → click Generate
  3. Copy the 16-character password shown (spaces are fine)
  4. Paste it into the App Password field above
